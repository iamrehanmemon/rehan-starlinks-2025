Section 01

Key Projects

LaunchPad · Legal Hub · Error Management · Email Framework · Internal Ticketing
LaunchPad
Customer Portal

Starlinks’ external-facing portal for all customers, providing a unified digital interface for onboarding, documentation, financials, analytics, support, and account management with role-based control.

  • Adaptive Home page for guest/pending/approved users.
  • Onboarding tracker with real-time status.
  • Document Center & Customer Master Mapping (Shipsy, Mintsoft, WMS etc).
  • Appendix Generator & Rate Card Ingestion (customer-specific pricing).
  • Finance module with D365 integration for KPIs.
  • Customer dashboards for real time analytics via Power BI.
  • Admin panel and role-based access across modules.
LegalHub
Internal Platform

A lightweight internal platform to manage legal requests at Starlinks.

  • Multi-Type Legal Requests — Contract Review, NDA, Legal Advice, etc.
  • Multi-Level Approval Workflow — Department → GM → MD → Legal.
  • Microsoft Teams Integration — Approve or reject with inline comments.
  • Outlook Email Approvals — Action approvals directly from email.
  • In-App + Email Notifications — Real-time updates with comments.
  • Form-Based Submission — With file uploads
  • Legal Team Directory
Error Management Automation
Internal Framework

Internal framework to monitor automation jobs and proactively notify developers when failures occur, enabling faster resolution and fewer escalations.

  • Automation failure detection & detailed logging.
  • Real-time monitoring of automation events.
  • Teams notifications routed to the right developer.
  • Extensible architecture to scale with future workflows.
Email Notification Framework
Reusable Engine

Template-driven email engine supporting real-time data injection, attachments, and trigger-based sends across automation platforms.

  • Reusable notification module across tools & flows.
  • Template-based emails with variable replacement.
  • Runtime injection of IDs, dates, and contextual data.
  • Auto attachments and event-based triggers.
Internal Ticketing System (Ongoing)
Support Platform

Centralized platform to manage Starlinks support requests (Procurement, HR, CX) with SLA enforcement, escalation logic, and routing.

  • Multi-parameter Routing.
  • SLA configuration with L1 escalations.
  • Automated L1 escalations on SLA Breach.
  • Event-based email notifications and Teams approvals for Legal.
  • Cross-departmental ticket submission workflow (In Progress).
  • Form-based submissions, canned responses, comments, and history logs.
Mini Project - ZATCA Compliance
DDU Invoice Automation

  • Delivered in just 2 weeks using Power Platform.
  • Automatically sends DDU invoices to consignees.
  • Over 1,000 DDU invoice emails successfully delivered to consignees in the first 14 days post go-live.
  • ZATCA-compliant and fully automated using the Email Notification Framework.
